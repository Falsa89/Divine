## Core Fantasy

“Evoca, fai ascendere e domina con il tuo roster leggendario.”

The game is a premium collectible gacha with a strong but secondary competitive component. The roster includes divinities, legendary heroes, mythological creatures, folkloric figures, titans, cosmic entities, angels/demons and personifications.

## Seven Pillars

1. Collezione Desiderabile
2. Identità Mitologico-Leggendaria
3. Presentazione Premium Elegante
4. Progressione Costante e Significativa
5. Competitività Forte ma Non Escludente
6. Battle Chiaro, Nobile e Leggibile
7. Gioco Vivo, Sociale e Community-Driven

## Visual Direction

- anime fantasy premium mobile gacha
- ornate but readable
- gold + deep blue/night blue UI identity
- no chaotic fantasy
- no fake text baked into assets
- face readability is mandatory in card/detail/fullscreen contexts

## Canonical Elements

Acqua, Fuoco, Terra, Vento, Fulmine, Luce, Oscurità

## Canonical Roles

Tank, DPS Melee, DPS Ranged, Mage AoE, Assassin / Burst, Support / Buffer, Healer, Control / Debuff, Hybrid Special

## Stable Rules

- Splash art is for UI/card/detail/fullscreen.
- Combat base/runtime sheets are for battle.
- Do not use splash in battle.
- Do not use combat_base as attack/skill/hit/death when state sheets exist.
- Runtime sheet heroes must not receive external BattleSprite motion.
- Every new hero must pass Combat QA Lab.
- Every new hero must have asset/UI/battle contract before being accepted.
